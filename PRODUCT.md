# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Ambitious remote candidates (editors, designers, copywriters, developers, operators),
with a dedicated South African talent-pool funnel. They arrive from social links and
referrals, size the company up in seconds, and either bounce or commit to a 15-minute
application. Mobile-heavy traffic.

## Product Purpose

Halevora's hiring funnel: careers site plus application engine. The design must do what
educate.io's does for Iman Gadzhi: make a lean agency feel like an empire, so strong
candidates invest real effort in a long application. The visual impression IS the
qualification tool; a flat page kills the funnel before question one.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic dark, quiet confidence, expensive minimalism. Three words: monumental,
disciplined, magnetic. Named reference lane: educate.io / Iman Gadzhi ecosystem (huge
Hanken Grotesk display type, Fragment Mono labels as a deliberate brand system, layered
metallic graphite depth with a cinematic blue grade, white pill CTAs in mono caps,
photography under blue-washed scrims, smooth reveal motion). Electric blue `--accent`
(#7d9bff) is Halevora's signature, tying labels and interactive states into the grade.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS careers page: white background, blue links, ATS-embedded job list.
- Bootstrap-dark admin gray; flat #111 panels with no depth or glow.
- Hype-bro landing pages: countdown timers, red arrows, screaming yellow highlights.
- Text-only "restrained" minimalism; this brand needs imagery and scale, not absence.
